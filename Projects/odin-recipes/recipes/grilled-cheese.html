Pesto Grilled Cheese

Description

I first had this pesto grilled cheese sandwich at a small restaurant and immediately went home to duplicate the recipe. I've been making my grilled cheese sandwiches this way ever since!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon softened butter, divided
  • 2 slices Italian bread
  • 1 tablespoon prepared pesto sauce, divided
  • 1 slice provolone cheese
  • 2 slices tomato

Steps

  1. Spread butter on one side of a slice of bread and place it, buttered side down, into a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread 1/2 of the pesto sauce on the unbuttered side of the bread. Stack provolone cheese, and tomato on top of pesto, in that order.
  3. Spread remaining pesto sauce on second slice of bread. Place it pesto side down onto sandwich in the pan. Butter the top side of bread.
  4. Gently fry sandwich, flipping once, until both sides are golden brown and cheese has melted, about 5 minutes per side.
